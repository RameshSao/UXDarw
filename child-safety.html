Child Safety Standards Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

The Teli Parivar App is committed to providing a safe, secure, and respectful environment for our community. Because our application includes matrimony and community networking features, we strictly enforce a zero-tolerance policy against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

1. Strict Age Requirement (18+)

The Teli Parivar App and its matrimony services are strictly intended for adults who are 18 years of age or older. The registration, profiling, or listing of minors (anyone under 18) is expressly forbidden. We actively monitor and remove any accounts suspected of belonging to or representing a minor.

2. Zero-Tolerance Policy

We have a zero-tolerance policy against any form of child exploitation, abuse, or grooming. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Uploading, sharing, or requesting Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Grooming or attempting to contact minors for inappropriate or sexual purposes.
  • Sexualizing minors in any text, images, profile descriptions, or community discussions.

3. Enforcement and Actions Taken

Any violation of this policy will result in immediate and severe action:

  • Immediate Termination: The offending user's account will be permanently banned and deleted without warning.
  • Reporting to Authorities: We will report any instances of CSAM or child exploitation to the appropriate law enforcement agencies and organizations, such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • Preservation of Data: We will preserve relevant user data as required by law to assist in criminal investigations.

4. User Reporting Mechanism

Community safety is our top priority. If you encounter any user, profile, or content that violates these child safety standards or appears to involve a minor, please report it immediately.

You can report suspicious activity directly within the app using the in-app reporting features, or by contacting our safety team directly via email.
