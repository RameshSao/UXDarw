Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our application (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

What We Collect

  • Location (approx. or precise, if you allow): Used only to provide location-based features and accurate timings. You can deny and set a location manually.
  • Device info (non-personal): Basic device/OS data used for app stability and update checks.
  • App usage (non-personal): Screen flows and crashes to improve reliability. No personal identifiers.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account sign-up; no email, phone, or name collected.
  • No background location tracking or real-time location storage.
  • No contacts, photos, or files.

How We Use Your Information

  • Compute timings and data based on your chosen or detected location.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and accuracy.
  • Deliver local notifications you enable (e.g., reminders, daily planner).

Data Storage and Retention

  • Calendar data is stored locally on your device for offline use.
  • Location values (city/coordinates) stay on-device; not sent to our servers.
  • Crash/analytics data, if enabled by platform defaults, is anonymized and used for diagnostics.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services / Play Store: app distribution and updates.
  • Android/iOS location services: to resolve your location when you allow it.
  • System notifications: for reminders and daily insights you enable.

Permissions We Request

  • Location (optional): To calculate accurate data based on location. Deny to use manual settings.
  • Notifications (optional): For daily planner and reminders.

Your Choices

  • Disable location permission and set a location manually.
  • Turn notifications on/off in Settings or system app settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data.

Security

We minimize data collection and use platform security best practices. No sensitive personal data is collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will update the “Last updated” date above when changes are made.
